####subject.number
Number 1-30, representing the ID of the participant in the study of 30 total participants

####activity
One of six activities recorded:
* walking - walking
* walking.upstairs - walking upstairs
* walking.downstars - walking downstairs
* sitting - sitting
* standing - standing
* laying - laying
